So what're we doin' ova here?

Our aim is to make the entire process of taking a business model online simpler for the local sellers. Just an excel sheet or pdf is enough for the site. By connecting the seller with the customers clustered by location and preference, we plan to make this process a whole lot profitable.

How it Works

Integrating local vendors and create a reliable network for local community of a particular town. We make the complicated hassle and process of taking your business online simpler than ever before.
How are we different?
  • We focus on the local shops and vendors. The needs and methods of a local shops are completely different from a huge wholesale markets. Local shops have a)limited products b) limited delivery range c) are habitual of their own personal space d)hesitate to mingle with technology and go through a cumbersome tiring process of uploading their products online
  • Our website's flexible back end allows it form various clusters based on location, sellers, product range etc.., thus opening more and more opportunities for the seller to expand.

The Catch

For the seller :
The seller can easily start off with his existing customer base and give them a better user shopping experience . The seller will slowly capture more customers online through native advertising which shall solely be handled by us.

The scope for growth is immense.
The seller can expand his business slowly at his own comfort by changing the delivery range and product parameters.
Indigenous local shops , artistic vendors , book hubs look for personalisation and we provide them with the same.

For the user :
Get your results based on the fastest delivery possible, different products at the particular online stores and much more. Experience visiting your market online from the safe comfort of your home.
Apart from all this, comes social distancing into play in the COVID crisis situation. The social distancing required during shopping from the local stores will come into play with pre-confirmation & payments of orders online form your local stores. The customers save time through quick, reliable purchases from the local vendors online.
Drive through is yet another valuable feature. Carrying all the items he needs in one go. Thus, saving time and maintaining social distancing.
